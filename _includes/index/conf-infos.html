The 7th edition of Incontro DevOps Italia (IDI2019) will take place in Bologna on Friday, March 8th. We are looking forward to meet you again this year!

Incontro DevOps is a 2-day conference about DevOps, Continuous Delivery, Cloud, Microservices, Automation, Testing, Big Data, Containers, Data Center Operating Systems, Software Operability, IT operating models and how to plan and organise IT Operations.

During the event, we will also host lightning talks and there will be an open space available. The day before the conference (7th of March 2019) our partners will present few workshops: more news will appear on Facebook and on Twitter (@incontrodevops).

Why join?

Che tu sia alle prime armi o un esperto del campo, non perdere l'opportunità di imparare qualcosa di nuovo dagli utilizzatori reali.

Avrai la possibilità di interagire con gli esperti che stanno utilizzando i container a servizio del business e della trasformazione digitale.
